EXPLORE US

FSW is a technical club of GRIET, supporting Free and Open Source Software tools, where students accomplish skills through the events conducted. Our aim is to encourage students to use free and open softwere tools for their programming endaevors we believe that knowledge and productivity should not be locked behind a paywall.

Vision and Mission

Our vision with this website is to connect club members in order to cultivate a community of collabaration , we want this website to act as a hub for developers to interact and connect with each other in order to eventually work on projects together.

Who are we?

What do we offer?

We offer a 24/7 group chat for club memebers to participate in discussions and collaborations with each other. a members list is available to enable discovery of individual users profiles , the profile contain their contact information.
